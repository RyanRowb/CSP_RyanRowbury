Hunting

The Start

As a wee little lad, I was taught that hunting was an important way to prove yourself as a man.
I still feel that point stands, and that hunting is one of the best options of recreation their is.
It bring you closer to your foodsource.

My first duck hunt at twelve.

Why I continue?

I find it fun to connect with nature and enjoy my freedom as an american. The fundimental belief
that hunting is "evil" is based upon wrong set morals. I ask you would you rather have less biodiversity,
more starvation (in animals), and more invasive/domintant species taking over your local scenery?

The Joys of Hunting

Hunting allows you to connect with your primal ancestors, and feel the roots of your life. Hunting also
alows you to see where some of your food comes from. Often we forget that the meat at the market isn't
just meat, it was an animal. Some people find this hard to swallow, they don't want to accept that for
human beings to live, animals must die. Hunting allows you to see that and respect the animals that
allow you to live.

To recap, hunting allows you to

  • Realize where your food is coming from
  • Connect with nature
  • Help you get outdoors
  • Teach important life lessons
  • Help you connect with your roots
  • and more!
